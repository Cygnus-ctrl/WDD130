Cat facts!
Cats only meow when trying to get a humans attention, they do not meow at other cats
Cat can jump 6 times their length
Cats have whiskers on their front legs

Things to help you raise your cat:
If you have multiple cats, get one litter box per cat
Some cats do not like to drink from bowls so buying a water fountain might be better for them
Cats have many food allergies, look up if a food is safe before feeding it to yoru cat
Spaying your cat can vastly extend your cats lifespan
